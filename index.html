About Us

Meet the Duo

The faces — and hands — behind every pour

CH

Carissa Hefta

Co-Founder & Lead Mixologist

Carissa brings USBG-certified craft and a decade of hospitality experience to every event. From intimate backyard gatherings to full wedding receptions, she has a gift for reading a room and building a menu that fits the moment. When she's not behind the bar, she's training for her next Tough Mudder — which tells you everything you need to know about how she approaches a challenge.

MT

Michelle Flannery

Co-Founder & Lead Bartender

Michelle spent six years as head bartender at Circles Waterfront Restaurant before trading the venue for the road. She brings deep roots in the Tampa Bay hospitality scene, a network built on trust, and the kind of calm, confident energy that makes every guest feel at home. She has been solo bartending private events for years — Heftology just gave her a home base.

Together, they've got your event covered — from the first consultation to the last call. 🍸
